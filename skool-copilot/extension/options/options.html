Copilot Settings

Backend (Supabase)

Your own Supabase project — run supabase/schema.sql there once. The same values are used by the web app.

Tip: with this extension installed, opening the Copilot web app syncs these settings over automatically.

AI provider (bring your own key)

Keys are encrypted with a device key and stored only in this browser — never sent to the backend. Calls go straight from your browser to the provider.

Your reply voice

Powers the side panel's Draft replies — no account needed. Paste 5–10 of your own real replies (one per line, or leave a blank line between longer ones). These few-shot examples are what make drafts sound like you.
